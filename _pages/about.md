### Engineering with Purpose
I am a 3rd-year Computer Engineering student at **Antalya Bilim University** and a recipient of the prestigious **Türkiye Bursları (YTB) Scholarship**. With a **3.84/4.00 CGPA**, I am deeply committed to the intersection of mathematical rigor and applied Artificial Intelligence.

### My Focus: AI for Social Impact
My passion lies in developing end-to-end Machine Learning solutions that solve real-world healthcare challenges. My flagship project, **MaRAI**, is a maternal health risk assessment tool that utilizes Random Forest classification to predict pregnancy complications with **99.6% accuracy**. 

I believe that the most powerful technology is the kind that bridges gaps—whether they are technical, clinical, or cultural.
